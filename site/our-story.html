Where It Started

A brand born from
a different standard.

We grew up in New Jersey. We know the Palisades — those ancient cliffs along the Hudson, steady and composed, indifferent to the noise across the water. That image stuck with us when we started thinking about what a cannabis brand could actually be.

Most of what existed felt wrong. Too loud, too gimmicky, too disconnected from how the product actually made people feel. We wanted something calmer. Something you'd actually want to carry.

"We didn't want to make another cannabis brand. We wanted to make the one we actually wanted to buy."

The Product

Designed to
disappear into your life.

The best products are the ones that fit. That sit cleanly in a pocket, look good on a table, and never make you explain yourself. That was the brief for every Palisades vape.

We obsessed over the hardware before we ever talked about strains. Four colorways — Forest, Terracotta, Midnight, and Stone — each a distinct mood, each designed to feel intentional rather than arbitrary.

The formulations followed the same logic: small range, clear effects, quality you can feel. No shortcuts, no filler. Just a clean 1.0g that does what it says.

New Jersey First

Built here.
For here.

We launched in New Jersey because this is home. Every dispensary partnership, every campaign, every decision has been made with this community in mind first.

Our Mission

Cannabis,
done differently.

Palisades exists to prove that cannabis can be refined without being cold, premium without being exclusive, and designed without losing its soul.

We're building a brand that reflects the people who actually use it — thoughtful, modern, and done with the idea that enjoying cannabis has to come with an asterisk.

This is just the beginning. New products, new partnerships, and more of New Jersey to come.
